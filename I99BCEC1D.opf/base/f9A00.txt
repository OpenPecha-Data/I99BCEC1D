nanv evaṃ saty asti grāho grāhyaṃ catad grahītā ca |pratiṣedhaḥ pratiṣedhyaṃ pratiṣeddhāceti ṣaṭkaṃ tat ||14||

ti pratiṣedhyaṃ yad idaṃ mithyāgrāho nāma, santi ca pratiṣeddhāro yuṣmad ādayo ’sya grāhasyeti siddhaṃ ṣaṭkaṃ |tasya ṣaṭ
kasya prasiddhatvād yad uktaṃ śūnyāḥ sarvabhāvā iti tad na |
