atha matam apekṣya siddhis teṣām ity atra bhavati ko doṣaḥ |siddhasya sādhanaṃ syān nāsiddho ’pekṣate hy anyat ||42||

athāpi matam apekṣya prameyan arthān pramāṇānāṃ siddhir bhavatīti, evaṃ siddhasya pramāṇacatuṣṭayasya sādhanaṃ bhavati |
